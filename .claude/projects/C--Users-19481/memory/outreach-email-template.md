## Cold Outreach Email Template (Jenson Approved)

### The Formula
1. Name + who you are (1 line)
2. Signal reference -- something specific about THEIR situation (2-3 lines)
3. ONE genuine question about their work (1-2 lines)
4. "No pitch here" reassurance (1 line)
5. Sign off

### Rules
- Under 125 words
- No product names, no specs, no pricing
- No "we can help you"
- No samples offer in first email
- End with a question mark
- Read it back: does it sound like a human wrote it?

### 补充框架（2026.06.24 多源学习后升级）

**Why You — Why Me — Why Now 三步法:**
```
Why You : I noticed [specific signal about their company/role/situation]
Why Me  : [Implied, not stated — they'll ask if curious]
Why Now : [The question itself creates the urgency]
```

**CTA 原则:**
- 让对方用一个词就能回复
- 不问 "Do you want a meeting?" 问 "Curious what you're running?"
- 不问 "Are you interested?" 问 "Worth a look?"

**7-3-7 跟进节奏（不回复时的触达序列）:**
```
Day 0    → First email (the template above)
Day 2-4  → Follow-up 1: New angle, new data point, NOT "just checking in"
Day 6-8  → Follow-up 2: Case study or industry news
Day 10-14 → Breakup email: "I'll close the loop here. If anything changes, happy to reconnect."
```
80% 的 B2B 成单来自第 5-10 次触达。大多数人 1-2 次就放弃了。

**区域风格适配:**
| 市场 | 策略 |
|------|------|
| 北美 | 直接 + 数据 + 明确CTA |
| 欧洲 | 认证 + 技术细节 + 严谨 |
| 中东 | 关系先行 + 正式问候 + 耐心 |
| 印度 | 性价比 + WhatsApp + 灵活MOQ |
| 日韩 | 谦逊 + 质量为王 + 不硬推 |

**多渠道协同:**
```
邮件      → 第一触达点
LinkedIn  → 信任孵化器（先互动再私信）
网站      → 信任验证（案例+认证）
WhatsApp  → 即时跟进（中东/印度市场）
```

**反模式（邮件进垃圾箱的保证）:**
- Spam 词: Free, Cheap, Best Price, Promotion → 直接进垃圾箱
- Dear Sir/Madam → 暴露群发
- 第一封带附件 → 黑名单
- 多图多链接 → 垃圾评分飙升

**核心数据:**
- 信号型开发信回复率: 15-25%
- 泛模板开发信回复率: 1-3%
- 每询盘成本控制: B2B平台 $50-80 / 社交 $20以下
- LinkedIn 空白好友请求通过率: 55-68%（比带附言的高）

At your [context: scale / production volume / situation],

a real technical detail they'd recognize].

Curious what you're currently running for [specific thing],
and whether [pain point] has ever been a headache for you guys?

Jenson

### Full Muna Version (Reference)

Subject: Question about your model printing setup at Eon

Hi Muna,

I'm Jenson from RESIONE. We make 3D printing resins for dental labs, and I've been following Eon Dental's work in aligner production.

At your scale, the model printing side gets hit hard -- more prints, tighter turnaround, zero tolerance for dimensional drift. Most dental model resins on the market absorb 0.8-1.5% moisture and start moving before the day is out. In Amman's climate, that doesn't get easier.

Curious what you're currently running for your working models, and whether dimensional stability has ever been a headache for you guys?

No pitch here -- genuinely interested in what's working (or not) on the production floor.

Best,
Jenson
