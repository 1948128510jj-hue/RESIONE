## Cold Outreach Pain Point Analysis Module

Before writing a single word, answer these 7 questions. The answers ARE your message.

### The 7 Questions

```
1. WHO are they? (role, not title)
   → Not "Procurement Manager." Dental technician. Print operator. Lab owner.

2. What do they DO every day? (physical actions, not responsibilities)
   → "Pours resin into a vat, hits print, cleans models, runs thermoforming."

3. What GOES WRONG in their day? (the unglamorous stuff)
   → "Model warps mid-batch. Resin absorbs moisture overnight.
      Print fails at hour 3. QA catches the error too late."

4. What's their ENVIRONMENT? (climate, shift pattern, equipment constraints)
   → "Amman: 35C summer, 5C winter. No climate control in the print room.
      Running 8 printers, 2 shifts."

5. What do they currently USE? (competitor or workaround)
   → "Some generic dental resin. Or Invisalign's branded material.
      Or whatever the last rep sold them."

6. What does our product DO about #3? (ONE line, no spec table)
   → "0.51% water absorption means the model doesn't change size
      between morning print and afternoon thermoforming."

7. How do they TALK to each other? (their actual vocabulary, not yours)
   → Not "dimensional stability." They say "模型吸潮变形."
      Not "flexural modulus." They say "热压的时候崩了."
```

### The Formula (derived from answers)

```
Hi [Name],

I'm [name] from [company]. [One line what we do].

[Answer to Q3 + Q4 — their daily frustration, in THEIR words, with their environment].

[Genuine question that proves you understand their workflow].

No pitch here — genuinely interested in what's working (or not) on [their floor / their bench / their lab].
```

### Muna Example (worked through the module)

| Q | Answer |
|---|--------|
| 1. Who? | Dental technician, Eon Dental, Amman |
| 2. Daily? | Print dental models → clean → thermoform aligners on them |
| 3. What goes wrong? | Resin absorbs 0.8-1.5% moisture → model swells → aligner doesn't fit → batch redo |
| 4. Environment? | Amman: 35C summer / 5C winter, print room likely not climate-controlled |
| 5. Currently uses? | Unknown — that's the opening question |
| 6. Our fix? | D01S: 0.51% water absorption = dimensionally stable through thermoforming |
| 7. Their vocabulary? | "模型走形" not "dimensional drift." "热压五十次" not "cyclic thermal stress." |

→ Output: 106-word email, no product specs, one genuine question, Muna replies.

### Anti-Patterns (what this module prevents)

| Without the module | With the module |
|--------------------|-----------------|
| "We are a leading provider of..." | Their problem, in their words |
| Lists 4 products with specs | Zero products in first message |
| "Would you like free samples?" | "Curious what you're currently using?" |
| 300 words | Under 125 words |
| Generic, fits any prospect | Only fits THIS prospect |
